SCP-173 [LEVEL 2]

SCP-173

Also called as "Peanut","Matthew","Nut"

CLASS: EUCLID

SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-173 is to be contained in a 50x50 sqaure feet containment chamber, personnel are to report scarping sounds from inside from time to time.

MTF Utilites: Mobile Task Force Epsilion-11 "Nine Tailed Fox" are equipped with a portable cage. View more info on [Containmaint Cage-173].

DESCRIPTION: SCP-173 is a statue made out of metal rebar and concerte. The anomaly is incapable of movement when in line of sight, when its broken (such as blinking, looking away, etc) SCP-173 "teleports" behind the victim's neck and cracks it.

Note: The Research Department of Site-91 calls it "teleporting" since it moves so fast that its like teleporting.

SCP-096 [LEVEL 2]

SCP-096

Also called as "Shy Guy"

CLASS: EUCLID

SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-096 is to be contained in a medium-sized titanium box equipped with multiple motion sensors, personnel are to report crying sounds from inside the box.

MTF Utilites: Mobile Task Force Epsilion-11 "Nine Tailed Fox" are equipped with Scrambler Goggles, preventing seeing the face of SCP-096. View more info on [Project-SCRAMBLE-096].

DESCRIPTION: SCP-096 is a tall, pale white, skinny anomaly. SCP-096 will stop at nothing to terminate anyone who sees its face, 096 also has superhuman strength that has managed to break through *all* know material.

Note: The Research Department of Site-91 recommends that SCP-096 be left alone at all times

SCP-079 [LEVEL 5]

SCP-079

Also called as "Sentient AI,"Computer"

CLASS: EUCLID

SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-079 is to be contained at Site-19. It's hardware restrictions are: An Intel i9 Processor, 1TB SSD, No GPU, 5TB RAM. No attempt must be made to connect SCP-079 to the facility's systems, doing so will resut in breach and possibly site-wide chaos.

MTF Utilites: Mobile Task Force XXXXXX "Sky Net" are equipped with a special terminator USB, when insertered, SCP-079 will be temporaily force-shutdowned. View more info on [079-Cont-USB]

DESCRIPTION: SCP-079 is an old macintosh computer likely created in the 1980s, tests so far conducted show that SCP-079 is a self-learning AI, collecting data ever since it's creation. SCP-079's anomalous abilites can still not be explained.

Note: The Research Department of Site-91 recommends not to talk or reference SCP-682 to him, doing so will cause 079 to ask for this wehereabouts.

SCP-008 [LEVEL 4]

SCP-008

Also called as "NOT FOUND"

CLASS: Pending Reclassification, old class; EUCLID

SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-008 is to be contained in a Gas Storage Compartment, as seen in Site-19.

MTF Utilites: Mobile Task Force Beta-7 "Maz Hatters" are equipped with Gas Masks, Oxygen Tanks and rarely a full Hazmat suit, preventing direct contact with the Gas. No extra Info available.

DESCRIPTION:SCP-008 is, presumably, a gas composed of various know & mostly unknown composition. When inahled, or made contact with the body, the subject will exprience coughing, vomitting, headaches, pain, etc until the subject dies.

Note: The Research Department of Site-91 advises all personnel present at any sort of testing with SCP-008 to be decontaminated.
